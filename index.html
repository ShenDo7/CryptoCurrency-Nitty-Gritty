Why Crypto?

A beginners guide to CryptoCurrency. Get all the data you need to get started without feeling overwhelmed.

So what exactly is CryptoCurrency anyway? It is a digital currency in which encryption techniques are used to regulate the generation of units of currency and verify the transfer of funds, operating independently of a central bank.
